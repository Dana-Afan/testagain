About Us

Below are some informations that can make you know us better:

CCC Is Known For Having Large Buildings

Cainta Catholic College is well-known for the buildings it has constructed for its students. Their structures are well-known and have a good view. The entire area is very open, making it ideal for walking and playing. Their buildings are well-built, and each one has a large number of rooms. It improves the overall appearance of the school. There are also buildings visible at the back of the school because it is very large. It is wide from the front, but it is even wider when you travel around the entire building.

CCC Is Known For The Our Lady Of Light Parish

Cainta Catholic College is well-known for the well-constructed "Our Lady of Light Parish." According to a blog, it is one of the most beautiful churches ever built in the Philippines. It is very elegant, as you can see from the outside. And so is the interior. When you enter the church, you will notice a large space that leads to the altar of God. The decors and cross-shaped structure make it very pleasing and appealing to the eyes. As a result, seeing this church is a huge deal. So come on in and attend a mass here every now and then!

Our School's Vision and Mission

Below is the Vision and Mission of CCC that includes details and promises of Cainta Catholic College to the students:

Vision

Cainta Catholic College envisions itself as the preferred educational institution and center for Religious Education in the Diocese of Antipolo with level 4 accreditation status.

Mission

Under the patronage of Mary, Our Lady of Light, Cainta Catholic College, commits itself to excellent learner-centered and technological-enabled programs and services with Religion at the core.
